Fertilizers provide the major nutrients (nitrogen, phosphorus and potassium and important secondary elements) that plants need. Unless the nutrients are replenished, the soil's productive capacity declines with every harvest.

Crop prediction in agriculture is complex, and various models have been developed. Factors like atmospheric conditions, fertilizer type, soil, and seed influence production. Multiple datasets are required to address this challenge effectively.
